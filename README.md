# 🚀 FinSight AI

## Predict. Protect. Prosper.

**Transforming Financial Data into Financial Intelligence using Artificial Intelligence**

## 📖 About

FinSight AI is an AI-powered Financial Intelligence Platform that helps students, professionals, and middle-class families understand, manage, and improve their finances using Artificial Intelligence, OCR, predictive analytics, and conversational AI.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Millions of people struggle to understand where their money goes because financial data is scattered across bank statements, UPI transactions, subscriptions, and bills. This leads to overspending, poor budgeting, and reduced savings.

FinSight AI transforms raw financial data into meaningful financial intelligence.
